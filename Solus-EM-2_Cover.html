Level up with proven health and wellbeing support
 

Help@hand from Unum provides total health and wellbeing support to employees, for vital support when they need it most.

In 2023 alone, over 100,000 consultations were booked with Help@hand services, and 92% of users felt that their clinician dealt with their problems during their appointment.

But it's not just the numbers -or the thousands of employees helped - which reveals the value to employers. It's the stories of those who have been supported that reveal how Help@hand helps employees manage personal challenges and move forwards.

“I felt I had, and still have, someone to talk to if I need to discuss something important that is troubling me. This means that I am happier and can spend my energy on more productive things instead of dwelling on negative emotions.”
